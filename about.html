About Me

My academic and professional journey

My name is Eduardo Pires, and I am an adaptable and dedicated professional currently transitioning into the world of software development.

My career path has been diverse, from working in elevator maintenance to hospitality and logistics, giving me a strong work ethic, resilience, and people skills.

I have always balanced my professional life with my passion for teaching, having been a combat sports coach since the age of 18.

I am fluent in Portuguese and English, with working knowledge of French and Spanish, and I am currently learning Japanese.

I enjoy learning new things, solving problems, and always strive for excellence in everything I do.

In my free time, I love being around animals, staying active through training, and exploring new ideas and skills.
